Schedule

DAY 1

Departure from Ahmedabad

Early morning departure. Visit Mount Abu en route. Reach Udaipur by night. Check-in and overnight stay.

DAY 2

Explore City Palace & Lake Pichola

City Palace tour, boat ride at Lake Pichola, Jag Mandir, local shopping.

DAY 3

Explore Fateh Sagar & Sajjangarh Fort

Fateh Sagar Lake, Monsoon Palace, Saheliyon Ki Bari, cultural evening at Bagore Ki Haveli.

DAY 4

Day Trip to Kumbhalgarh

Full day trip to Kumbhalgarh Fort, light trek and return to Udaipur by evening.

DAY 5

Return Journey to Ahmedabad

Local breakfast and souvenir shopping. Departure to Ahmedabad. Arrival by evening.
